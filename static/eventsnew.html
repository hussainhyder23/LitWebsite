Bhaashothsav

Click here to Register

“Bhashotsav – Bhasha wohi, soch nayi” is an exclusive Hindi language based event organization as part of literati X. It reflects on the importance of Hindi as a widespread Indian language and how its awareness uplifts knowledge and promotes belongingness to this country. The event will comprise two rounds, the first, a preliminary elimination round related to general awareness and associated questions. As the difficulty of the questions increases, participants will be permitted to avail lifelines for assistance. The second round, however, is a fun filled, interactive round that is associated with Bollywood and requires one to step up with various personal skills. The aforementioned rounds will be judged without bias and the winners will have exciting takeaways waiting for them at the finish line.
